Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

FuelTracker is an iOS application developed by BB Labs.

Information We Collect

FuelTracker collects only the information you choose to enter into the app, including vehicle names and fuel fill-up details such as dates, trip miles, gallons purchased, and fuel prices.

The app uses anonymous authentication provided by Google Firebase. No usernames, passwords, email addresses, or personal identifiers are required.

How Data Is Used

Data is used solely to provide FuelTracker’s core functionality, including syncing fuel records across devices within a shared household.

Data Storage

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore. Data is accessible only to devices associated with the same household configuration.

Data Sharing

FuelTracker does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Data Retention

Data remains stored until you choose to delete it from the app.

Children’s Privacy

FuelTracker does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
